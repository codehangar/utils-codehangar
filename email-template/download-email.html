  • Installing the app and connecting your database instance(s)
  • Viewing and paging through your data
  • Sorting by specific columns (fields) in table view
  • Inline edit (for string and integer fields only) in table view
  • Insert and update records
  • Delete records

We look forward to hearing about your experience with the new app. As you find problems or bugs, please use the menu items within the app to send us any feedback!
